Lassiter, Daniel. 2017. Graded Modality. Oxford University Press.
	http://web.stanford.edu/~danlass/Lassiter-book-draft.pdf

Lewis, David. 1973. Counterfactuals. Blackwell.

Judea Pearl. 1988. Probabilistic Reasoning in Intelligent Systems: Networks of Plausible Inference. Morgan Kaufmann.
